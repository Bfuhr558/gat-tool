GAT
Is your service working for women?
This tool helps organizations working with people experiencing homelessness understand whether their services meet the needs of women — including during menstruation and menopause. It takes about 10 minutes and ends with a tailored portrait of your service and concrete next steps.

GAT is part of Blossom's WeCare program. WeCare supports homeless service organizations in Denmark and the Nordic countries to provide dignified, gender-sensitive care — starting with the most basic and often overlooked needs.

Your responses are not stored or shared. This tool is for your own reflection and development.
